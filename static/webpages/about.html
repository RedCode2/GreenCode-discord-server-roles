About us

This website is made for explaining GreenCode's discord server's roles and their functions. Every role is explained in detail except the Administrator, Moderator, Bots and Green Code (not Green Coder) roles. If you have any doubts regarding any roles or the server itself, then feel free to DM the moderators of the server. We are always there to help you. Just don't spam to us, and we will help you out.

Rules of the server

  • Be respectful: Please be respectful to everyone in the server. Whether be it a friend you know physically or any stranger whom you don't like. Being disrespectful to others can result in a ban.
  • No spamming: Please don't spam the same message over and over again in any text channel.
  • No textwalling: Please don't textwall in general chatting channels. Textwall means to cover a large portion of the page just by a single message.
  • No offensive language: Please don't use any offensive language/images in the server. Doing this can result in a ban without any warning or a second thought by the moderators/admininstrators.
  • No offensive names/Profile pictures: Please don't use any offensive names or profile pictures in the server. The staff members will ask you to change your name/profile picture if it is disturbing.
  • No Direct & Indirect Threats: Threats to other users of DDoS, Death, DoX, abuse, and other malicious threats are absolutely prohibited and disallowed.
  • Follow the Discord community guidelines: Please follow the discord community guidelines. You can find them here.
Your presence in our server implies accepting these rules, including all further changes. These changes might be done at any time without notice, it is your responsibility to check for them.
