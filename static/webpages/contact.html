Contact Us

If you have any questions/concerns or suggestions then feel free to contact me or the other moderators through the following methods:

  • Discord username: red__code
  • Github username: RedCode2

Don't just contact me! There are other moderators who can help you with your questions or concerns regarding the server. However, if you have concerns regarding this project itself, then you can contact me.

Want to contact us? Just follow the following rules to message us:

  • Be polite and respectful to us.
  • Please don't spam the same message over and over again.
  • Just be straight to the point. Don't just make out an entire story just for a short suggestion.
  • Please don't request to be a moderator/admin.
  • Please don't ask for personal information or sensitive information.
