Professional

The Professional role is challenging to get. We have a test to see if you deserve this role or not. Click on this link to take the test. Good luck!

Meme Lord

If you send memes regularly in the memes channel, you will get the Meme Lord role. Post memes regularly and the mods will find out if you deserve this role.

Patreon / Donor

The Patreon role is only for those who are Patreon members of GreenCode whereas the Donor role is only for those who donated to GreenCode. Click on this link to view the links to donate to GreenCode or become a Patreon member of GreenCode.

Server Booster

The Server Booster role is only for those who boost the server. Boosting a discord server can improve the features in the server like 'adding more space for maximum number of custom emojis' etc.

2 Focus

The 2 Focus role is for those who selected it. It basically provides you information and updates about the 2 Focus app. You can report bugs on the app, suggest features and many more. You can install the 2 Focus app on Play Store (android) or the App store (iOS).

Green Coder

The Green Coder role is the basic role for everyone! Whenever a user joins GreenCode's server, they will be given this role. Somehow, if you don't get this role, and it's been a few days since you joined the server, feel free to contact the mods.

Question of the Day

The Question of the Day role is for those who selected this role. Whenever a question is asked in qotd, the members of this role is pinged. A bot asks the question, and the members of this role answer it in a specific channel.

Other roles

Other roles include roles like 'AI', 'Python', 'C/C++', 'Javascript', 'Web Design' and 'Game Creation'. When you join the server, you can choose what you're good at from the following, then the roles are added to your profile. Yes, you can choose all of them even if you're not good at them. Yup, it's so simple and easy!
